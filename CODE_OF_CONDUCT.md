# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a positive experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes personal attacks, trolling, harassment, and publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **info@fourninecloud.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
